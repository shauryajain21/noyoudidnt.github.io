Setting the Stage

comedians and entrepreneurs, social activists and industrialists, influencers, and writers people from each and every domain come here to tell their story but with a twist . Everyone is 5 years back, exactly where you want to start. Although we start with knowing more about our amazing guests, gasping and reacting ‘no you didn’t’ on their stories, we slowly move on to a chapter where they tell what they would have done 5 years ago with their (yours?) interests . We also talk about the more general things like their best or worst experience, easiest and hardest challenges, and what was something they just did right so you have a knowledge-packed fun experience.

Hey everyone I am shaurya, sitting on my desk with a shin chan figurine at the right side of it and a Walter Isacson biography on its left I am here to be a mediator between the brightest minds of the 21st century and you. I love minions and crypto.

Everyone is on a journey, just on a different stage. ‘No, you didn’t’ podcast brings you episodes with people from all stages some who are in the beginning, some who are in the middle, and some who are closer to the end. The people at the beginning will inspire you to start, the people in the middle will inspire you to continue and the people closest to the end are the ones whose journey you will look forward to. We hope you’ll stick around with us in our journey. ;)
